Ukuze ufinyelele umgomo wakho wokonga, udinga ukonga amanye amarandi angamakhulu amane nambili ngaphezulu. Ibhange likukhokhise amarandi angamashumi ayisithupha nesikhombisa ngalesi sikhathi. izindleko zakho eziphakeme kakhulu imali yesikolo, igrosa, uphethiloli nomshuwalense.
