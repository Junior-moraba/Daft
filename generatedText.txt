I-akhawunti yakho inamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amatatu nambili. Ususondele ekuhlangabezaneni nesabelomali sakho, ukunethezeka kudlule inani elibekiwe ngamarandi angamashumi ayisikhombisa . Ngenxa yesiyalo sakho sokonga unamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amabili  ongawasebenzisa ngendlela othanda ngayo. Ibhange likukhokhise amarandi angamashumi ayisithupha nesikhombisa ngalesi sikhathi. Unganciphisa ukusebenzisa kwakho imali futhi wandise nemali oyongayo ngokunciphisa  umshuwalense, uphethiloli, igrosa nemali yesikolo uma kungenzeka.
