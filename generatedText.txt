unebhalansi yamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amatatu nambili. Ususondele ekuhlangabezaneni nesabelomali sakho, ukunethezeka kudlule inani elibekiwe ngamarandi angamashumi ayisikhombisa . weqe umgomo wakho wokonga ngamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amabili . Ibhange likukhokhise amarandi angamashumi ayisithupha nesikhombisa ngalesi sikhathi. izindleko zakho eziphakeme kakhulu umshuwalense, uphethiloli, igrosa nemali yesikolo.
