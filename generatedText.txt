Njengamanje unamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amatatu nambili kwi-akhawunti yakho yasebhange. Ususondele ekuhlangabezaneni nesabelomali sakho, ukunethezeka kudlule inani elibekiwe ngamarandi angamashumi ayisikhombisa . Ukuze ufinyelele umgomo wakho wokonga, udinga ukonga amanye amarandi ayizinkulungwane ezimbili namakhulu ayisikhombisa  ngaphezulu. Ibhange likukhokhise amarandi angamashumi ayisithupha nesikhombisa ngalesi sikhathi. Unganciphisa ukusebenzisa kwakho imali futhi wandise nemali oyongayo ngokunciphisa  umshuwalense, uphethiloli, igrosa nemali yesikolo uma kungenzeka
