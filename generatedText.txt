Njengamanje unamarandi ayikhulu namashumi amatatu nambili kwi-akhawunti yakho yasebhange. Ucishe wahlangabezana nesabelomali sakho sokusebenzisa imali kodwa ukusebenzisa imali ezintweni zaokunethezeka kudlule isabelomali ngamarandi angamashumi ayisikhombisa . Ukuze ufinyelele umgomo wakho wokonga, udinga ukonga amanye amarandi angamakhulu amane nambili ngaphezulu. Ibhange likukhokhise amarandi angamashumi ayisithupha nesikhombisa ngalesi sikhathi. Unganciphisa ukusebenzisa kwakho imali futhi wandise nemali oyongayo ngokunciphisa  imali yesikolo, igrosa, uphethiloli naomshuwalense uma kungenzeka.
